“Bridging the Gap: The Philippine Digital Divide”


A project that explores the digital divide in the Philippines, focusing on differences in access to digital technology and how these disparities affect social class, ethnicity, geography, and underdeveloped communities. The project includes a Figma infographic and a website prototype.
